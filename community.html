Community Builder

Building communities is my favorite activity. I enjoy helping users in need and warning those who do not follow the rules. Discord is the platform I use most frequently, but I also contribute on Telegram and Twitter. I have been involved in building communities for several projects. Among these projects, I have earned the OG role as a reward for my contributions. Some of the common tasks I perform to build and maintain communities include:

  1. Communicating with other users
  2. Avoiding discussions about other projects within a specific project
  3. Assisting users who need help
  4. Reporting users who do not adhere to the rules
  5. Warning users who violate the rules
  6. If I have the authority, I will issue timeouts or remove roles according to the policies.

My involvement in these activities has allowed me to foster a positive and supportive environment within the communities. I take pride in creating spaces where users feel welcome, informed, and engaged. My contributions have not only helped these communities grow but have also strengthened my skills in community management and moderation."
